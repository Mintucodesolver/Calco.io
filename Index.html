About BMI Calculator

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a measure of body fat based on height and weight. It helps categorize individuals as underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese. Note that BMI is a screening tool and not a direct measure of body fatness.

BMI Categories:
Underweight: < 18.5
Normal weight: 18.5 - 24.9
Overweight: 25 - 29.9
Obesity: ≥ 30

About Mortgage Calculator

The mortgage calculator helps you estimate your monthly mortgage payments based on the loan amount, interest rate, and loan term. This tool is useful for home buyers to plan their budget and understand their financial commitments.

Formula used: M = P [ i(1 + i)^n ] / [ (1 + i)^n - 1]
Where M = monthly payment, P = loan amount, i = monthly interest rate, n = number of payments
